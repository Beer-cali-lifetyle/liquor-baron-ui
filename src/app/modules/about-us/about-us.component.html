About our company

Founded in 2019, we launched our first store located in Cochrane, and later expanded our illustrious operations to Airdrie. Our aim is to offer the Alberta libation community an unparalleled selection of wines, spirits, and craft beers that cater to the discerning palates of our esteemed clientele. We take pride in our commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer service, which is reflected in our collection offerings and the shopping experience we provide.

Cultivating Experiences

Through our carefully curated events, you'll deepen your knowledge and appreciation of the craftsmanship that goes into every bottle. Whether you're a seasoned connoisseur or new to the world of libations, our tastings and events are tailored to provide you with an experience that is both educational and enjoyable.

Crafting Memories

The perfect bottle can elevate any events, from a birthday party to a retirement bash. We believe that every bottle has a story to tell, and our engraving service allows you to add your own unique chapter. With our help, you can create a meaningful and memorable experience that will be treasured by you and your loved ones for years to come.

Fostering Community

We understand that the enjoyment of spirits, wine, and beer can bring people together. That’s why we strive to create a welcoming environment where enthusiasts can gather and engage in an experiential shopping experience. Through various initiatives, such as our wine club, we strive to create a sense of community where like-minded individuals can come together to share stories, tips, and recommendations.
